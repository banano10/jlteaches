The Compromise of 1850


Back to Social Studies


Was about the following:

  • Five bills proposed by Senator Henry Clay of Kentucky to resolve arguments over whether post-war
    new territories would be slave states
  • Kept slavery legal but eradicated slave trade in Washington D.C.
  • Admitted California as a free state
  • Left Utah and New Mexico to decide whether they were free states or slave states
  • Made it easier for slave owners to catch runaway slaves
  • Defined new boundaries for the state of Texas
  • Congress debated Clay's proposal for seven months
